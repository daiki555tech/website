ABOUT THE GAME

Story

In the medieval village of Skyhoot Ruins, a brave owl guardian takes flight to protect the stone courtyards. With sharp aim and fast wings, you fly, dodge, and shoot through waves of enemies in a fun retro pixel-art top-down shooter.

Game Genre

Top-down Shooter • Pixel Art • Medieval Action • Single Player

HOW TO PLAY

Controls

Movement

Use W, A, S, D keys to move your character around the map.

Aim

Move the mouse to aim where your character will shoot.

Shoot

Click the left mouse button to fire at enemies.

Power-Ups

Health

Restores a portion of lost HP to help you stay longer in battle

Attack

Deals double damage to enemies to eliminate them faster

Gameplay Tips from Kuwago

▸ Keep moving to avoid attacks.
▸ Grab power-ups to stay strong.
▸ Target nearest enemies first.
▸ Use walls for cover.
▸ Watch health and retreat when needed.
